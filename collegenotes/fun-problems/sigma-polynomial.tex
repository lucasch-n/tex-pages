\maketitle

Problem: Given an non-zero, n-variable, complex-valued polynomial of finite degree $\mathcal{P}$, prove that $\mathcal{P}(m, \sigma(m),\dots,\sigma_{n-1}(m))$ is not identically zero at all values of $m$ for $m\in\mathbb{N}$.
